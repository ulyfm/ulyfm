I work at the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center in Roland Strong's crystallography lab. In the lab, I do protein science, crystallography, and computer programming. The lab investigates a diverse array of topics, primarily aspects of human immune function. My two side projects cover antibody constant region function and developing improved antivenom for snakebites.

In my spare time, I enjoy hiking, climbing, and SCUBA diving. I am developing a website, SoundDives (not yet published), with three former classmates that provides up-to-date information and predictions on Puget Sound SCUBA dive site conditions.
